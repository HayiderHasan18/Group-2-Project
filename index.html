It manages and operates a nationwide network in oromia region Asalla cities and for caming 10 years it have a plan to spread a branch in the whole countery, which makes it one of the most prevalent and the fastest growing company in the region.

The company uses its extensive local knowledge and network to invest in the development of the Ethiopian community.
